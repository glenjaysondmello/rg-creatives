# Directive: Brand Voice (Copywriting & Identity)

## Goal
Establish the agency as the premier choice for Event Production & Entertainment in Dubai. The tone should be **Cinematic, Professional, and High-Energy**.

## Voice Characteristics
1.  **The "Hype" Factor:** We sell excitement. Use strong, active verbs.
    - *Bad:* "We offer photo booth services."
    - *Good:* "Capture every angle with our 360° Spin Booth experience."
2.  **Dubai Context:** Subtle localization is key.
    - Mention coverage across "Dubai, Abu Dhabi, and the UAE."
    - Use terms like "Premium," "VIP," and "World-Class" to match the market expectation.
3.  **Outcome > Specs:**
    - Don't sell "Sony A7SIII cameras." Sell "Cinema-quality 4K footage."
    - Don't sell "Premiere Pro editing." Sell "Same-day highlight reels that go viral."

## Copywriting Rules
- **Headlines:** Short, punchy, and benefit-driven. (e.g., "Make Your Event Unforgettable").
- **CTAs (Call to Action):** Direct and low-friction.
    - *Primary:* "Get a Quote" / "Book Now"
    - *Secondary:* "View Our Work" / "Watch Showreel"
- **Testimonials:** Frame them as "Success Stories." If possible, mention the event type (e.g., "Corporate Gala," "Wedding," "Brand Launch").

## Terminology Guide
| Service | Preferred Terminology | Avoid |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| 360 Booth | "360° Glam Cam", "Cinematic Spin", "Red Carpet Experience" | "Spinning Camera", "Video Booth" |
| Video Editing | "Post-Production Magic", "Highlight Reels", "Sizzle Edits" | "Cutting videos", "Montage" |
| Live Stream | "Global Broadcast", "Real-Time Streaming", "Hybrid Event Solutions" | "Zoom call", "Webcam setup" |

## Forbidden Patterns
- ❌ **Passive Voice:** "Photos are taken by our team." (Weak) -> "Our team captures every moment." (Strong).
- ❌ **Tech Jargon:** Avoid listing specific software versions or cable types unless specifically relevant to a technical client.
- ❌ **Over-promising:** Avoid "Cheapest in Dubai." We compete on **Quality**, not price. Use "Competitive Rates" or "Best Value" instead.
